Naturopathic medicine is a distinct form of primary care that combines traditional healing practices with modern medical science. Naturopathic doctors (NDs) are trained to diagnose, prevent, and treat disease using natural therapies including clinical nutrition, botanical medicine, acupuncture, and lifestyle counseling. We focus on treating the whole person and addressing root causes rather than just suppressing symptoms.
