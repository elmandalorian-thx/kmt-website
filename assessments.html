Assessment length varies by type. Neuropsychological assessments typically require 4-8 hours over 1-2 sessions. Psychoeducational assessments usually take 3-5 hours. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your initial consultation and work with your schedule to minimize disruption.
Many assessments are covered under extended health benefits, WSIB, MVA insurance, or through school board funding. We provide detailed invoices for insurance submission and can help you understand your coverage options before you begin.
You'll receive a comprehensive written report with findings and personalized recommendations. We also offer a feedback session to discuss results and answer questions. Our reports are accepted by schools, employers, and insurance providers, and include practical strategies for moving forward.
Psychoeducational assessments focus on learning and academic functioning--ideal for identifying learning disabilities or ADHD. Neuropsychological assessments provide a deeper look at brain-behavior relationships across multiple cognitive domains, making them ideal for complex cases, brain injuries, or neurological conditions.
Some components of assessments can be completed virtually, though many standardized tests require in-person administration for validity. We offer flexible scheduling including evenings and weekends, and virtual follow-up sessions are available across Ontario.
