Who Benefits from the EmpowerHer Program

The EmpowerHer Program is designed for women navigating the complexities of midlife transitions who are seeking comprehensive, expert-guided support.

🩺

Managing Physical Symptoms

If you're experiencing:

  • Hot flashes and night sweats
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Weight changes and metabolism shifts
  • Joint pain or muscle aches
  • Changes in energy levels
💭

Navigating Emotional Changes

If you're dealing with:

  • Mood swings and irritability
  • Anxiety or depression
  • Brain fog and memory concerns
  • Loss of confidence
  • Identity shifts and life transitions
🌟

Seeking Comprehensive Support

If you want:

  • Expert medical guidance on hormone therapy
  • Mental health support from therapists
  • Holistic wellness approaches
  • A supportive community of women
  • Personalized care that addresses your unique needs

Midlife can feel overwhelming. Between hot flashes, mood changes, sleep disruptions, and the emotional weight of transitioning through perimenopause and menopause, many women feel lost, alone, and unsupported.

The EmpowerHer Program offers a different path. This is not just another wellness program--it's a comprehensive 8-week journey that brings together medical experts, mental health professionals, and holistic practitioners to support every aspect of your midlife transition.

Whether you're just beginning to experience symptoms or you're deep in the midst of change, EmpowerHer provides the medical knowledge, therapeutic support, and community connection you need to thrive during this transformative phase of life.

Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions Answered

What age range is the EmpowerHer Program designed for?
The EmpowerHer Program is designed for women typically between ages 40-60 who are experiencing perimenopause or menopause symptoms. However, every woman's journey is unique. If you're experiencing symptoms of hormonal transition at any age, we encourage you to reach out to discuss whether the program is right for you.
Is hormone replacement therapy (HRT) mandatory in the program?
No, HRT is not mandatory. While we provide comprehensive information about hormone therapy options and can prescribe HRT if it's appropriate for you, all treatment decisions are personalized based on your unique health profile, preferences, and goals. Our physicians will work with you to determine the best approach, which may or may not include HRT.
Can I continue with my existing therapist or doctor?
Yes! The EmpowerHer Program can complement your existing care. We encourage open communication with your current healthcare providers and can coordinate care to ensure a comprehensive approach to your wellbeing. Our team is experienced in collaborative care models.
How much time commitment does the program require?
The program is designed to fit into your busy life. You can expect:
  • One weekly therapy session (50 minutes)
  • Medical appointments as needed (initial assessment plus follow-ups)
  • Scheduled treatments (IV therapy, aesthetic services) at your convenience
  • Optional group activities and workshops
Most participants dedicate approximately 2-4 hours per week to the program.
Is the program covered by insurance?
Some components of the program may be covered by extended health benefits, including psychotherapy sessions and certain medical consultations. We provide detailed receipts that you can submit to your insurance provider. We recommend checking with your insurance company about your specific coverage. Our team is happy to provide the documentation you need for insurance claims.
When does the next cohort start?
We run EmpowerHer cohorts multiple times throughout the year to keep group sizes intimate and ensure personalized attention. Contact us at (905) 829-5686 or reception@kmthealth.com for information about upcoming start dates and available spots.
What happens after the 8-week program ends?
Your journey doesn't end after 8 weeks! You'll receive a personalized wellness plan for ongoing care, lifetime access to the EmpowerHer alumni community, and the option to continue with any of our individual services. Many graduates choose to maintain relationships with our therapists, physicians, and wellness practitioners for ongoing support.
